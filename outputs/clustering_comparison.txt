=== DBSCAN vs K-Means for Seismic Hotspots ===

DBSCAN is generally better suited for earthquake epicenter clustering because:
1. It does not require specifying the number of clusters in advance.
2. It can find arbitrarily shaped clusters (fault lines are often linear/curved, not spherical like K-Means assumes).
3. It has a notion of 'noise' (cluster -1), which handles scattered, independent seismic events naturally.
